How It Works

From source to qualified target.
Four steps. No manual cleanup.

01
Connect Your Sources
Plug in SAM.gov, SerpAPI, Apollo, or a custom endpoint. Bring your own API keys. Designed for local-first and direct BYOK workflows.
02
Define Targeting Rules
Set geography, NAICS codes, industry, company size, and opportunity type. Rules bind the AI -- no drift into bad-fit targets.
03
Verify and Score
9 runtime qualification gates check every target before it enters your pipeline. Bad data, hallucinated contacts, and undeliverable emails are rejected at this stage.
04
Route Approved Targets
Qualified targets push directly to your CRM, Instantly campaign, or outreach sequence. No copy-paste. No manual export. Pipeline moves.

Why not just use Apollo,

The Problem With
Apollo + SerpAPI
They surface contacts. They do not qualify them. Bounced emails, chain locations, and contacts without decision-making authority all flow into your CRM unchecked.
SourceDeck gates every contact before import
The Problem With
Airtable Alone
Airtable stores data. It does not verify it, score it, or enforce follow-up discipline. Your base fills with stale, unqualified records that degrade every report you run.
SourceDeck validates before records ever land
The Problem With
ChatGPT for Prospecting
Generative AI produces plausible leads -- not verified ones. Email domains get transposed. Businesses get fabricated. Without runtime checks, you are chasing hallucinations.
SourceDeck runs corroboration on every output
The Problem With
Instantly Without Qualification
Sending unverified contacts burns your domain reputation. A single bad batch can destroy months of warmup. If contacts going in are not clean, the campaign cannot save you.
SourceDeck approves before push, not after bounce
